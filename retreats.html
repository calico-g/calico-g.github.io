About the location

Beautiful Camp Sealth is located on nearly 400 acres of incredible forest and wetlands on the southwest shore of the magnificent Vashon Island, Washington. The property includes over a mile of gorgeous saltwater beaches along Colvos Passage.

The main camp area, right on the water, features a large play-field, dining hall, campfire amphitheater, beautiful views, plants and fresh sea air. Enjoy near-pristine forests while hiking miles of trails along and within the wetlands and waterfront. Relax, refresh and revive in this revitalizing outdoor environment, a perfect setting for those who appreciate a relaxed wilderness atmosphere.

Classes and meals will be held in the spacious main lodge with wood floors and big windows that bring the outdoors in; this is the essence of the best of Pacific Northwest life! Group sleeping accommodations in cabins clustered throughout this amazing north woods setting provide you the chance to really experience this incredible natural location while connecting with others attending the camp.

Enjoy delicious food prepared by our own personal chef, able to cater to any of your specific dietary needs.
